# The Date Crew Design System

## Brand Identity
A premium, exclusive matchmaking service for relationship-ready professionals. The aesthetic is mature, elegant, and concierge-like. It should feel like a high-end country club or luxury service, avoiding modern "dating app" tropes (no hot pink, no swiping interfaces).

## Colors

### Primary Palette
- **Brand Dark Green** (`#1b3a2f`): The core background color for headers, important cards, and heavy UI elements.
- **Brand Medium Green** (`#244c3c`): Used for primary buttons and secondary backgrounds.
- **Brand Accent Green** (`#386058`): Used for inner shadows, borders, and subtle accents.

### Accent Palette
- **Brand Bronze/Gold** (`#dc9e4a`): The primary accent color for icons, text highlights, and active states.
- **Dark Gold** (`#7D5115`): Used for hover states and deeper emphasis on gold elements.

### Neutral Palette
- **Off-White/Cream** (`#fdfbf7`): The main background color for the application to provide a warm, organic feel compared to harsh pure white.
- **Pure White** (`#ffffff`): Used for card backgrounds.
- **Slate Gray** (`#475569`): Used for secondary text.
- **Dark Text** (`#1e293b`): Primary text color for readability on light backgrounds.

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Playfair Display`, serif. Used for page titles, card headers, and large numbers. Exudes elegance.
- **Body & UI Elements**: `Inter Tight` or `Roboto`, sans-serif. Used for data tables, forms, buttons, and badges. Clean and highly legible.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary Button**: 
  - Background: Linear gradient from `#244c3c` to `#1b3a2f`.
  - Text: White, font-weight 500.
  - Border Radius: Fully rounded (`rounded-full`).
  - Shadow: Outer shadow `rgba(37,37,39,0.15)` and an inner shadow `inset 0px 1px 2px 2px #386058`.
- **Secondary Button**:
  - Background: Transparent with a `#dc9e4a` border.
  - Text: `#dc9e4a`.

### Cards
- **Style**: Pure white background with a subtle drop shadow (`shadow-md`).
- **Border**: Very subtle light border, or no border with just shadow. 
- **Corners**: Rounded corners (`rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`).

### Badges
- Used to indicate customer status (e.g., "Match Sent", "New Lead").
- Style: Pill-shaped (`rounded-full`), using soft background colors with dark text, or utilizing the Bronze/Gold for emphasis.

## Layout & Spacing
- Spacious and breathable. Avoid dense, cluttered layouts.
- Use ample padding inside cards (e.g., `p-6` or `p-8`).
- Clean separation of sections using soft gray dividers.
